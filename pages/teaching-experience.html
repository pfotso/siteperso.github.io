Teaching experience

Sciences et sociétés

Many pedagogical objectives are associated with this course : the exploration of socio-scientific controversies, project-based pedagogy but also scientific mediation for doctoral students on their research subject. Thus, to conclude this reflection, a day of restitution in the form of 1h30 workshops is organized. It is intended for Master students in order to focus their theoretical research on practical answers. It is a course in scientific mediation that allows the dissemination of knowledge to both an expert and a lay audience.

Supervision of Master students, academic year 2017-2018, IUEM, U.B.O.
